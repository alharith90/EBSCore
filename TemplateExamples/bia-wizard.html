Suggested Data Extensions
Optional relational tables to enrich dependency, equipment, infrastructure, system, and continuity strategy capture.
Table Purpose Key Fields
BIA_Dependency Stores supplier and internal service dependencies with SLA alignment to RTO/RPO. BIAID (FK), DependencyName, Type (Supplier/Application/Infrastructure), RTOAlignment, Owner
BIA_Equipment Captures critical equipment and quantities required during disruption. BIAID (FK), EquipmentName, Quantity, RecoveryLocation, Notes
BIA_Infrastructure Documents facility utilities such as power, HVAC, connectivity, access controls. BIAID (FK), Service, Provider, RecoveryTime, GapFlag
BIA_System Lists applications and integrations required for MBCO with interface details. BIAID (FK), SystemName, DependencyType, RPOHours, RecoveryPlan
BIA_BCStrategy Summarizes chosen continuity approach and testing cadence. BIAID (FK), StrategyType, Description, TestDate, Owner
Example: Dependency alignment query
-- Locate dependencies where recovery commitments exceed the process RTO
SELECT d.DependencyName, d.Type, d.RTOAlignment, b.RTO, d.Owner
FROM BIA AS b
JOIN BIA_Dependency AS d ON b.BIAID = d.BIAID
WHERE b.CompanyID = @CompanyID
  AND b.IsDeleted = 0
  AND d.RTOAlignment = 'Gap';
